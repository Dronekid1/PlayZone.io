What is Parsec?

Parsec is a powerful remote desktop application that lets you stream games and applications from your computer to any device. Perfect for accessing your gaming PC from school, work, or anywhere with an internet connection.

How to Use

1. Click "Launch Parsec" to access the web interface
2. Sign in or create a free Parsec account
3. Connect to your home computer or join a friend's session
4. Enjoy gaming remotely with low latency streaming
